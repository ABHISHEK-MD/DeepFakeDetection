Deepfake Detection

From Fake to Real

A deepfake refers to a manipulated or synthesized media, such as images, videos, or audio recordings, created using deep learning techniques. The term "deepfake" is derived from "deep learning," a type of artificial intelligence (AI) that involves training neural networks with large datasets to perform specific tasks, such as image or speech recognition.

Spotting Deepfakes

Educate individuals on how to recognize signs of deepfakes. This could include inconsistencies in facial expressions, unnatural movements, or artifacts in the content.
